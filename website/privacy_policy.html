Privacy Policy

Welcome to the privacy policy of the game "Slendertubbies," created by seba0456. This document outlines the procedures for collecting, using, and safeguarding player data. By using the game, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Purpose of Data Collection

I want to be transparent about the objectives behind collecting player data. The primary reasons for data collection are as follows:

  • Database Management: "Slendertubbies" is an amateur solo project, and the data collected helps in honing database management skills. It provides insights into effective data organization and manipulation.
  • Enhancing Player Experience: I analyze player preferences and behaviors to improve the gameplay experience. Your data helps me tailor the game's elements to align with your preferences.
  • Gameplay Adjustments: Understanding player activity informs me about in-game dynamics. This information is vital for fine-tuning gameplay features and creating an engaging environment.

2. Collected Data

When you use the "Slendertubbies" game, I collect the following data:

  • Player's nickname
  • Used game version
  • Registration date
  • Last activity timestamp
  • Played matches
  • Completed matches
  • Collected custards
  • Catched survivors
  • information if player have ever used Epic Online Services

3. Public Player Profiles

To foster a sense of community, specific information such as your nickname, played matches, and collected custards may be displayed publicly on my website. However, you have the freedom to control the visibility of your public profile through account in-game settings, giving you the ability to limit the display of this information.

4. Data Sharing

I hold your data in high regard and want you to know that I don't share it with any third parties, individuals, or entities. Selling player data is not a practice I engage in.

5. Data Security

It's important to me that your data remains secure. As an amateur solo project, the developer takes care for ensuring the confidentiality and safety of your data. Industry-standard security measures are implemented to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

6. Use of Epic Online Subsystem

The "Slendertubbies" game uses the Epic Online Subsystem for online matchmaking features.

7. Contact me

If you have any questions or concerns regarding my privacy policy, please feel free to contact me at my Discord Server.
